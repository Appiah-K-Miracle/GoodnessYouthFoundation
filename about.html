Our Mission

Mobilizing Youth from the underprivileged backgrounds and offer them vocational and technical skill training through the informal sector to equip them with employment skills

  • How we operate:Mobilizing youth especially girls from disadvantaged backgrounds to give them employable skills training
  • Paying periodic working visit to the apprentices at their various places of work to ascertain how they fare regarding their state of health, regularity and punctuality to work.
  • Organizing periodic guidance sessions for the apprentices as part of their capacity building through the use of resource personnel from diverse profession and vocation
  • Mobilizing funds from person to organize graduation for the apprentices at the end of their apprenticeship

Our Vision

Helping Youth to be economically independent through employable skills.

Our History

Goodness Youth Foundation was founded by Mr. Amos Eshun with a mission to empower vulnerable youth, People With Disabilities (PWD), and orphans aged 15 to 30 through skills training. Recognizing the challenges faced by disadvantaged young people, the Foundation focuses on vocational and technical training to create employment opportunities and economic independence. Since its creation, Goodness Youth Foundation has mobilized youth, supported apprenticeships, provided mentoring, organized equipment donations, and celebrated graduations. Guided by compassion, purpose, and a commitment to uplift, we continue to build a future where every young person has the skills and confidence to succeed.
